Documentation

Learn how to fully leverage the GST Compliance System to manage your tax invoices and returns seamlessly.

Getting Started

First, create an account using your business email. Once logged in, navigate to the Dashboard where you can set your GSTIN and business profile credentials.

Managing Transactions

Under the Sales Invoices and Purchases tabs, you can log all GST-relevant transactions. The system will automatically compute CGST, SGST, and IGST based on the entered HSN/SAC codes.

Generating Returns

Navigate to the GST Returns module. The system securely aggregates your previously logged invoices to produce actionable GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B summaries that you can review and export for final filing on the government portal.
