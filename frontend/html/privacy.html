Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

We log generic business registry and tax identity data explicitly submitted through our interfaces, including but not limited to business aliases, GSTINs, invoicing data, contacts, and account administration emails.

How We Use Information

All data collected is programmatically manipulated solely within the context of generating automated GSTR returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) and reconciling ITC claims. We do not sell or leverage your tax data for third party commercial endeavors.

Data Security

Your transactions and user state are secured via database-level cryptographic hash validation, persistent stateless JWT authentication logic, and rigorous parameter filtering.
