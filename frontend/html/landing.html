FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the GST platform.

Is the platform compliant with the latest GST rules? +
Yes. We regularly update our logic and HSN/SAC databases to align precisely with the latest Indian GST rules and IT matching regulations.
Can I generate both B2B and B2C invoices? +
Absolutely. Our invoice module natively supports B2B, B2C, and RCM compliance, calculating separate SGST, CGST, and IGST based on place of supply.
How does automated reconciliation work? +
By uploading your GSTR-2A/2B JSON files, the system automatically cross-references your logged purchase invoices to highlight missing ITCs or value mismatches instantly.
